About Play Work ☰

Hello, I’m Ashley Chen.


I love the intersection of design, politics, media, and ever-changing popular culture. I've always been eager to dive deeper into the human psyche and the evolution of technology has allowed that passion to grow endlessly.

Today, I am a UX designer and I solve human problems by being someone that is empathetic, collaborative, and solutions-minded.

Being involved in emerging technology, speaking on behalf of equality and systemic issues, and generally being a better person for myelf, and others, empowers me. I'm a sponge when it comes to learning new, exciting things. I take pride in knowing that my designs are always in beta, and tomorrow, they will be better than the day before.

If I'm not taking pictures on film like my mom did in the 90's, you can find me curating extremely specific playlists and ripping them off Youtube circa 2008, dreaming about exploring a new country, watching Ted Talks and crime documentaries, and tending to my two (very needy) cats. Eventually, I'd like to learn ceramics and raise a blooming teenage plant successfully.
